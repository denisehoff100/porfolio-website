About Me

My Story

I am currently living on the Gulf Coast of Florida. My professional life began in NYC where I was fortunate to work for companies that allowed me to work and travel to places around the world: Mumbai and Chennai, India, Singapore, and Hong Kong.

My free time is divided amongst many creative outlets. I have played piano my whole life so that has been a ‘friend’ each day. When I can find extra time, sewing a new wardrobe is always enjoyable!

A fun fact about me?

Years ago I played in the pit on Broadway. And, before that, stitched costumes for Phantom of the Opera!

My first job was with McGraw-Hill Publishing in NYC which is where I learned to creatively collaborate with a fantastic group of people. I learned by doing. Eventually I decided to try freelancing. One client brought many more, and before I knew it, I had a company which grew from a small design and production studio in NYC/FL to a larger facility in India. Rather than outsourcing, we became the ‘outsourcer’. Whew! Lots of stress overseeing a large team across the ocean! Through that experience I realized that people, no matter what country you are in, all strive for the same thing in life. Purpose. After 14 years I chose to return to music, as a musical director and teacher at a prep school. I continued to keep my hand in design, and recently realized that my true calling is that of a designer. So, I took the plunge and embarked on a boot camp course bringing me full-circle, back to what I loved at the start of my career, visual design.

Why me?

I love being a part of the creative process. Learning keeps me excited about the future. I am a focused worker but also able to be a part of a large team and thoroughly enjoy working with groups around the world. Having been a teacher, I offer a unique insight regarding how users’ have different learning needs.
